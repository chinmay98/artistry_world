About

"ARTISTRY WORLD" is a platform acknowledging the artists around the globe. We here provide you information about various painting styles which were quite famous in 18-1th century. We also provide you information regarding artists practising those styles and all about their career and their different artworks. We here provide a platform to all the people who love paintings and want some inspiration.
Here is a list of painting styles and thier respective artists which we have covered:
